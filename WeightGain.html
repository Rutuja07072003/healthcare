“To keep the body in good health is a duty, otherwise we shall not be able to keep our mind strong and clear.” – Buddha

- If you want to gain weight, focus on healthy muscle and fat gain rather than unhealthy belly fat.

- Eat nutrient-rich foods in sufficient quantities, maintain a calorie surplus, and combine it with light workouts for balanced weight gain.


- Weight Gain Diet Chart -




- Consume high-protein meals, complex carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

- Include nuts, seeds, dairy products, eggs, lean meat, legumes, and whole grains in your diet.

- Eat frequently throughout the day (5-6 meals) and maintain hydration for optimal digestion and nutrient absorption.
