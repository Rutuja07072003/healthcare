- 7 Day's Diet Plan For Weight Loss -


- A structured 7-day diet plan helps reset your eating habits and encourages mindful food choices. This plan focuses on fresh fruits, vegetables, controlled portions, and proper hydration to naturally support weight loss.

- The goal of this diet is balance and discipline. By following proper meal timing and avoiding junk food, your metabolism improves and your body starts burning stored fat efficiently.

- Along with light exercise and sufficient water intake, this plan can help you feel lighter, more energetic, and refreshed by the end of the week.

DAY 1 – Fruits Only

- Eat all fresh fruits except bananas.
- Watermelon, apple, papaya, orange, and berries are recommended.
- Drink 8–12 glasses of water.
- Avoid any other food.

DAY 2 – Vegetables Only

- Eat boiled, steamed, or raw vegetables.
- Start your day with one boiled potato.
- Include green vegetables like spinach, cabbage, carrots, beans.
- Drink plenty of water.

DAY 3 – Fruits & Vegetables

- Combine fruits and vegetables.
- Avoid bananas and potatoes.
- Maintain proper hydration.
- Helps boost metabolism.

DAY 4 – Bananas & Milk

- Eat 8–10 bananas.
- Drink 3 glasses of milk.
- You may include light vegetable soup.
- Stay hydrated.

DAY 5 – Rice & Tomatoes

- Eat 1 cup brown rice.
- Include 6–7 fresh tomatoes.
- Increase water intake to 12 glasses.
- Avoid processed food.

DAY 6 – Vegetables & Rice

- Eat vegetables and 1 cup rice.
- No oil cooking.
- Balanced nutrition for strength.
- Drink sufficient water.

DAY 7 – Rice, Vegetables & Juice

- Eat brown rice and vegetables.
- Drink fresh fruit juice (no sugar).
- Complete detox cycle.
- Continue healthy habits after plan.
