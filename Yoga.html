“It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver.” – Mahatma Gandhi

- Yoga is one of the best ways to stay physically and mentally healthy. Regular practice helps reduce stress, improve flexibility, enhance concentration, and increase overall energy.

- It is suitable for all ages and requires dedication, focus, and awareness of breathing, posture, and movement.

- By incorporating yoga into your daily routine, you can maintain balance in both body and mind while preventing lifestyle-related disorders.



-Examples of Yoga-


1. Pranayama

- Pranayama is the practice of controlling the breath. It helps calm the mind and enhances the flow of life energy in the body.

- Useful for stress relief, improving mental clarity, and maintaining physical balance.

2. Vrikshasana

- Vrikshasana (Tree Pose) improves balance, posture, and concentration. It strengthens the legs and core while calming the mind.

3. Dhyana Virasana

- This seated posture is excellent for meditation, relaxation, and mental clarity. Comfortable to sustain for longer periods without strain.

4. Ushtrasana

- Ushtrasana (Camel Pose) improves back flexibility, core strength, and stretches the front of the body.

5. Shirshasana

- Shirshasana (Headstand) enhances balance, focus, and strength. Supports both mental and physical stability.

6. Shavasana

- Shavasana (Corpse Pose) is used for relaxation and integration of yoga practice. Helps calm the mind, reduce stress, and restore energy.

7. Surya Namaskar

- Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation) is a sequence of postures performed in a flowing sequence to honor the sun. Enhances flexibility, strength, and overall vitality.
