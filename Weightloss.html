“Take care of your body. It’s the only place you have to live.”

- Weight loss is not about starving yourself. It is about making healthier food choices and staying physically active every day.

- Start with small, realistic goals. Even losing 5–10% of your body weight can improve heart health, reduce the risk of diabetes, and boost your overall confidence.

- Focus on balanced meals, proper hydration, and regular workouts rather than quick-fix diets. Consistency is the key to long-term success.
