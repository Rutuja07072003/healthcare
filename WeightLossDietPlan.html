- Balanced Indian Diet Plan for Healthy Weight Loss -


- This balanced Indian diet plan focuses on portion control, high-protein meals, fiber-rich vegetables, and adequate hydration. Instead of eliminating traditional Indian foods, the goal is to modify cooking methods and choose healthier alternatives.

- The key to weight loss is consistency. Eating small, balanced meals every 2–3 hours helps regulate blood sugar levels and prevents overeating.

- Along with this meal schedule, include 30 minutes of walking or light exercise daily for best results.
